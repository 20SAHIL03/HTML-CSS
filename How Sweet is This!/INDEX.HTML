About Candy

Gummy bear candy is a chewy, gelatin-based candy that is typically shaped like a bear. It comes in a variety of flavors, including fruit and sour varieties. Gummy bears are enjoyed by both children and adults and are a popular treat around the world. It consists of a jelly center with fruit flavors covered in sugar. It was first introduced in the United States in 1985.

Ingredients:

  • Modified Food Starch
  • Fructose
  • Water
  • Gelatin
  • Natural and Artificial Flavors
  • Color Added (Includes Red 40, Yellow 5, Blue 1)
  • Citric Acid
  • Dextrose
