ASUS Z790/H770/B760 Motherboards

ASUS and ROG Z790, H770, and B760 motherboards promise full support for 13 th Gen Intel ® Core™ Processors, with even higher clock speeds, more cores and support for faster DDR5 memory than ever before. The ROG, ROG Strix, TUF Gaming, Prime and ProArt series are prepared for this onslaught with enhanced performance tuning, cooling, power delivery, connectivity, and ease of installation.

Exclusive Features

ASUS MultiCore Enhancement

Unleash the power of an Intel 13th Gen CPU with ASUS MultiCore Enhancement, all while keeping temperatures under control. This UEFI BIOS setting helps boost all CPU cores to max turbo speeds. New for Z790, ASUS motherboards offer an additional option which similarly removes the limits holding back CPU boosting behavior, but also imposes a CPU temperature limit at 90° Celsius. This gives you outstanding multicore performance, but with restrained thermals.
